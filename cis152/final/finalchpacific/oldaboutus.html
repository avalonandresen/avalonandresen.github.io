Meet Your New Paradise

Our Mission

Our mission at Pacific Trails Resort is to provide our guests with an unforgettable experience that combines breathtaking natural beauty with exceptional service and amenities. Pacific Trail Resort strives to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere where guests can relax and recharge in a luxurious and peaceful environment.

Our Commitment to our Future

At Pacific Trails, we believe in creating lasting memories for our guests through personalized service and attention to detail. We aspire to be the premier luxury resort in the Pacific Northwest, and we are committed to continuously improving and innovating to exceed the expectations of our guests.

What should you expect?

Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a corporate retreat, we have the perfect accommodation to suit your needs. At Pacific Trails, our resort is designed to operate in harmony with the surrounding ecosystem, utilizing green technologies and responsible practices to preserve the natural beauty of the Pacific Northwest for generations to come. Our on-site dining options offer guests a variety of delicious cuisine prepared with the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients. Access to amenities such as, local trails, kayak rentals, yoga or bird watching, provide guests with the opportunity to relax and rejuvenate during their stay.
